Sahara Aamby Valley

Total Water-scaping of the project of Sahara spreading over 10000 acres. We have delivered the total turnkey solutions for the water body and water fountains. Spanning over glamorous area of Lonvala, AAMBY VALLEY is a huge landmark in India. We have done the total waterfall solutions, Floating fountains, Landmark fountains and entrance fountains. Our fountains have been built for the capacity spanning over 800 HP of submersible pumps having a huge creation of water beauty!

Sant Dnyaneshwar Udyan, Paithan

Neltronics has done a total turnkey development of Paithan garden of 365 acres having an exclusive span of water fountains, water body, water pools and Musical Fountains. A wide range Apollo type, bubblers, ring type fountains, mist fountains and many more with an consumption of 350H.P submersible motors.

Cidco Belapur

The fountain project of CIDCO Belapur is a combination of different fountains installed in the Belapur area. The fountains at the signal ofBewlapur id a combination of Bubbler fountain along with a sheet type fountain. This fountain has a cloud effect at the bottom. The other fountain is a series of jets installed in a circular pond. Adjacent to this fountain is a fountain in which there is a center jet which had been accompanied with cascading waterfall and small bubblers. The pathway at the top has got thick bubblers series.

DSK Super Infocomm

DSK Superinfocomm is a mega institute of animation, gaming and designing on Solapur Road, Pune. Neltronics has built a spectacular fountain at the entrance pathway of the institute. The fountain comprises of a series of milky jets (bubblers) fountain. The series of bubblers is followed by a large sheet waterfall which falls into the lower pond and thereby creates a majestic feel. The lower pond is given a cloudy effect by using mist fountain. The length of the fountain is approx. 150 feet and has got a mega consumption. The fountain is illuminated with bright colors and hence creating a vibrant atmosphere around the fountain.
