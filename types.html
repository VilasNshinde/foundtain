Welcome to Neltronics

Large Scale Fountains

The large scale fountains involve all the mega scale fountains that we have installed in different locations depending on the area and the best combination of fountains which would suit the ambiance. The large fountains require a fairly large size of area and generally are designed to have a great view from a long distance too.

View Gallery

Medium Scale Fountains

The medium scale fountains are the fountains which are installed in a decent size locations wherein the space needs to be added with a beauty by using a water body. This work is done perfectly by medium scale fountains which would make the fountain increase the utility of the space available.

View Gallery

Small Scale Fountains

These fountains are generally indoor fountains and are installed inside a closed premise. The fountain involves more of a aesthetic value, and is combined with a very mild flow of water which creates a soothing ambiance and sparkles up the indoor ambiance.

View Gallery

Glass Fountains

We at Neltronics believe in synergy of two elements and creating a unique marvel piece! Glass fountains is an unique blend of glass and water which offers a unique, yet prestigeous water effect in indoor ambience. we have created enormours types of glass fountains, which have been a true landmark and have added to the beauty of many places.

View Gallery

Musical Fountains

A musical fountain, also known as a dancing fountain, is a type of animated fountain for entertainment purposes that creates an aesthetic design. This is achieved by employing the effects of timed sound waves and timed light against water particles.
